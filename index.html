Custom plugins

Havana expands on the towny/earth experience with some unique plugins.


TownyDecay

Land that is interacted with will gradually restore if it is not claimed.

BorderLoop

Automatically teleports you to the other side of the world when walking to the edge.

TownyTrade

Use custom spawners to generate items that are unique to your biome/area.





Rules

Full Terms of Service

Users must follow the rules at all times or they risk a potential punishment to their account.


All punishments are handled on a case-by-case basis. Breaking a specific rule may not always result in the same punishment every time. When investigating situations, context is the most important factor.


§ A. Chat


§ A1. Users are protected by free speech and may give their positive or negative opinions on users, so long as that opinion is not discrimination or repeated harassment against the user.


§ A2. Users may not share personal details of other users, including real names, locations, emails, phone numbers, or any other information that may be considered personal. Users may release their own information at their own discretion as long as that information is not of malicious intent.


§ A3. Users may not send repeated messages of the same (or similar) text, or flooding chat with meaningless text.


§ A4. Users may not be annoying or a nuisance to the chat. This rule is up to the moderator's discretion.


§ B. Content


§ B1. Users may not send illegal content.


§ B2. Users should not link to unreputable, illegal, or sources that may phish other users.


§ D. Promotion & Advertising


§ D1. Users may promote their own content, such as websites, channels, videos, or live streams, as long as it is relavent to the conversation or server and not random advertisement.


§ D3. Users must bear in mind § B2 when promoting content.


§ E. Use of Service


§ E1. Threats of any kind against the user or the service will result in a permanent ban.


§ E2. Users may not use modifications such as spam bots, hacked clients, XRay, etc. Acceptable client modifications may include OptiFine and Shaders.
